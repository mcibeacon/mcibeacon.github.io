    Article of Awesome

    Sanaa Mahmud Instagram Nov 03, 2019 · 1 min read
    Share this

    Although school is important, it can get quite long and repetitive sometimes. It’s the same classes every other day for 10 months straight, so we tend to get stuck in a boring routine: school, homework, sleep, repeat. It's difficult to find pleasure in the school stuff we do, so we turn to other things, like entertainment, media, sports, and the arts. So, while you’re sitting in whatever class right now, here are 15 awesome everyday things to think about. I hope they may cheer you up, or make your outlook on life a little more positive, without the feelings of stress, anxiety and craziness.

    1. Sleeping in a bed with fresh sheets
    2. Looking in the fridge/pantry and seeing your favourite snack
    3. The feeling of a soft rug under your bare feet 
    4. The buzz after you’ve just posted a new picture on social media, and see the positive comments start rolling in
    5. The smell of freshly baked cookies
    6. Finishing that thing you’ve been procrastinating about for a long time 
    7. Naturally waking up early, without an alarm 
    8. Finally eating the food you’ve been craving forever
    9. When someone asks, “How was your day?” and you actually have good/eventful news to share 
    10. Having a good hair day 
    11. Cracking a joke and making everyone laugh
    12. When you randomly reach out to a friend to see if they’re free and they are
    13. The movie theatre lights dimming, letting you know that the magic is about to begin
    14. Being the one in your friend group who actually remembered the homework
    15. The feeling of walking out of school on a Friday 

    There you have it! Just a few ordinary things that may make you smile, or fill you with satisfaction. I hope these things give you the motivation to conquer your day and remind you that there’s a lot to enjoy in the world, so keep a lookout!
